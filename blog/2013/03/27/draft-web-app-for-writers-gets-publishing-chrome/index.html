Draft, Web App for Writers, Gets Publishing, Chrome Extension in Recent Update

Mar 27th, 2013 | Comments

Draft can now publish to your blog — that’s how I published this post. It also has a new Chrome extension that makes version-controlling and saving your writing on the web even easier.

This is big. I’ve mentioned Draft before and it’s already my favorite way to compose text on the web. Developer Nate Kontny has even open-sourced the Chrome extension so anyone with the chops can modify it. If you haven’t check it out, give it a go at draftin.com.

You can save notes to Dropbox, Google Drive, Box.com, Evernote, or your local hard drive, not to mention publish directly to a Tumblr or Wordpress blog. It’s a wonderful experience from the first draft to hitting “Publish” and I look forward to future updates.
